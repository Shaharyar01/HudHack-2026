FAQ
Do I need cyber security experience?
No. The event is open to all backgrounds. We have beginner-friendly challenges and mentors on standby.
Can I start coding beforehand?
No. All project code must be created during the event timeframe. Brainstorming and wireframing beforehand is permitted.
What equipment do I need?
Please bring your own laptop and charger. Power strips and Wi-Fi will be provided at the venue.
What if I don't have a team?
Don't worry! We can help you find a team on our Discord server beforehand, or we will help you find teammates on the day of the event.
How do teams work?
Teams can have between 1 and 6 members. You can also hack solo if you prefer the "lone wolf" approach.
Can non-Huddersfield students attend?
No. This event is exclusive to students currently enrolled at the University of Huddersfield. You will need your student ID for entry.
Is food provided?
Yes. Food and refreshments will be provided for all attendees. Specific details on the menu and timing will be confirmed closer to the date. We will accommodate dietary requirements where possible.
